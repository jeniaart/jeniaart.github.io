Evgeniya Gutova

I'm Evgeniya (Jenia) Gutova, a linguist deeply engaged in the study of Berber, though I often get sidetracked by other languages that catch my curiosity.

I earned a BA in Cuneiform Studies and Languages and Cultures of Mesopotamia and Anatolia at Leiden University in the Netherlands, followed by an MPhil in Linguistics there, focusing on Field Linguistics and Berber Studies. In 2021, I finished my PhD in Linguistics at the Sorbonne in Paris, where I studied Senhaja Berber from Northwestern Morocco.
